Understanding soil chemistry & parameters

N

Nitrogen (N)

Essential for vegetative leaf growth. Promotes lush green coloring and synthesis of vital plant proteins.

P

Phosphorus (P)

Critical for root network building and flower budding. Empowers the transfer of energy through cells.

K

Potassium (K)

Drives plant disease resistance, cellular water regulation, and improves overall crop resilience.

pH

Soil pH Balance

Controls chemical solubility. A neutral range (6.0 - 7.0) typically provides the highest nutrient bioavailability.
